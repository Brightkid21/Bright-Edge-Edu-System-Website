Activity-based Pedagogy and Enriched Engagement

90% of a child’s brain development happens before the age of 5. (source: Harvard Centre of Developing Child). Children have a natural desire to learn. Dr. Maria Montessori recognized that children have an intrinsic motivation to learn even and engage in games and activities, children learn to grasp an object, they learn to stand by trying again and again and they master walking.

Physical development activities not only make the concepts more tangible but also appeal to children's diverse learning styles and take advantage of their multi-sensory strengths. If children are physically involved in doing activities, they are more apt to be mentally engaged.

Entire Teaching Content, Resources are just a Click or Scan away with Anytime, Anywhere Teacher

Parents get a Virtual-Teacher on the pages of the books and worksheets that we provide in our book kit. My Bright Kid books are unique and are Augmented Reality (AR) enabled which delivers rich interactive content online focused on curriculum-based education.

With images, alphabets, words, rhymes, stories being precisely projected and narrated just like having a real-teacher do the teaching, early childhood learning and concept building are strengthened and accelerated in our preschool programs. We are the first in the world to innovate to add this technology feature to Montessori and Preschool Learning materials that we make.

Award-Winning Curriculum and Innovative Teaching

Any good education program always needs a Well-founded, Effective, Age-appropriate Curriculum and a delivery program that infuses the urge of learning making the curriculum easy and fun-filled. Our award-winning curriculum has developed more than 35,000 children which is time tested and has the benefit of 14+ of our teaching experience of running hundreds of Preschools across the country with the academic partnership.

We believe “ Adding knowledge is Creating Potentials ...Applying Knowledge is the True Power” Our activity-based learning approach not only makes any learning long-lasting but also enhances a child’s ability to apply the knowledge. The innovative learning approach part of our curriculum is completely based on experimental hands-on learning; which is well-balanced and designed to stimulate each child’s physical, social, cognitive, and emotional development.

World Class Hands-on Learning & Montessori Materials in our Kits

Hands-on learning & creative play are important ingredients for learning. Dr. Maria Montessori felt strongly that the hand and brain must develop in harmony. Our hands-on Montessori materials are well crafted mostly by using natural materials like wood and boards.

These are categorized into few activity types such as: up to Playgroup (age up to 3 years) - Eye-hand coordination, Music and Movement, Practical Life, Arts and Crafts and Language. For Children of age group 3 to 6 years- Exercises of Practical life, sensorial, Language, Arithmetic, and Enrichment. Children in preschools and at home love not only exploring but also repeating the activities. Montessori and other materials that we would provide are made of natural materials like wood and boards which are long-lasting for much-repeated use.
