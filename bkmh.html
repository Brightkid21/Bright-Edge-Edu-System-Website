Activities

At Bright Kid Montessori House learning is always fun, concepts are always deep-rooted, applications are always encouraged, and creativity is always championed.

Classroom Activities

Our classroom activities prepare our children in their regular class activities through various worksheets, pattern writing, maths, drawing & colouring and class interactions. This is the traditional method of learning.

Montessori Activities

The Montessori activities in our preschool is a hands-on approach using scientific materials that stimulate creativity in the absorbent minds of children through their exploration of many possibilities and develops the children in 5 key areas of knowledge- Exercises of Practical Life, Sensorial, Language, Maths and Enrichment.

Interactive Smart Class

The Interactive Smart Class activities make learning fun-filled in our preschool. Over 1000 Interactive Smart Class Activities for teachers to use in the classroom which is vital for preparing the child for tests as they progress through school and to take up challenges and improve Logical ability,
Problem solving, Communication skills and Teamwork.

Thematic Activities

Thematic activities in the preschool strengthens creative thinking and fosters the conversion of “thoughts” into “feelings”. And feelings are better sensed and remembered forever. Our Thematic teaching covers various age-appropriate topics and stories for children. It is a medium to inculcate moral values, like sharing, respecting & caring among children.

Montessori Learning Edge

Children develop life long love for learning. Our Montessori environment provides the children hands on wooden materials and activities suited to the child's intellectual, emotional, physical and social needs at each stage of development. Montessori activities help multi-sensory hands-on minds on activities for cognitive development. The 9 areas of development are:

  • Eye Hand Coordination
  • Solving puzzles
  • Cognitive skills
  • Motor skills
  • Building Blocks and manipulatives
  • Exercises of Practical Life (Daily life skills)
  • Sensorial
  • Language
  • Arithmetic

Benefits of Montessori Method

Montessori method of education can significantly help the children to learn. They become independent, enthusiastic, and self-directed learners at the same time they will also learn the skills necessary to be successful academically and in life. Here are the key benefits of of choosing Montessori education for children:

  • Precise and scientific Montessori materials invite Exploration.
  • Develops Sense of order.
  • Increases creativity skills.
  • Helps repetition for refinement of concepts.
  • Enrichment of vocabulary.
  • Concrete concepts of numbers and quantities.
  • Phonemic awareness needs for both reading and writing.
  • Self-respect and respect for others.
  • Problem solving.
  • Sense of a community.
